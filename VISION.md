# Project Vision: The AI Boardroom

## The Problem with Single-Agent AI
Current AI tools suffer from "yes-man" syndrome. When a user asks a single AI assistant a question, the AI generates a response based on a generalized dataset. It rarely challenges the user's core assumptions, nor does it view the problem from multiple conflicting, highly specialized viewpoints. 

## The MeetingHall Philosophy
MeetingHall operates on a fundamental principle: **Collaborative intelligence is superior to isolated intelligence.**

The most successful enterprise decisions, product launches, and creative works do not happen in a vacuum. They happen in a boardroom where the Engineer challenges the Designer, the Marketer challenges the Musician, and the Strategist balances the budget. 

## The AI Boardroom Concept
MeetingHall replicates this dynamic. When a meeting is transcribed, it is not merely summarized. It is passed into the AI Boardroom.

1.  **Specialization over Generalization:** MeetingHall deploys discrete AI models configured with strict personas (e.g., a highly technical Systems Architect, a user-focused Product Designer).
2.  **Deliberation:** The models review the meeting transcript. The Engineer might flag a technical impossibility, while the Business Strategist identifies a market opportunity.
3.  **Consensus:** The **Consensus Engine** forces these models to resolve their contradictions before presenting a final output to the user.

MeetingHall transitions AI from a simple "answering machine" into a robust, analytical team.
